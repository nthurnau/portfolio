Background

I am a New Jersey native who had a ten year stint in Boston and now lives in sunny Los Angeles. As a developer with a teaching background and a Masters Degree in educating deaf students, I am enthusiastic about using my skills to solve problems in real-time. My time as a teacher has helped me develop a keen sense of when things aren’t going the way they are supposed to and how I can find a quick solution. I find the challenge of revising and fixing as fun and satisfying as quick success. In addition to my actual teaching skills, I have honed and crafted my ability to fluidly code-switch between different languages. I learn new languages quickly and best through immersing myself in them. I enjoy finding patterns, acknowledging the differences, and playing with language by means of hyperbole. Like education, web development is an ever-evolving field: there are always new rules to follow, new trends to explore, and new things to learn. I find this exciting and look forward to being a part of the changes that are to come.
